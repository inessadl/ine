About me

I'm a UX/UI Designer based in Porto Alegre, Brazil. I'm currently working for the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP). I believe that transparency, collaboration and diversity are the keys to build an awesome product.

I have a Bachelor Degree in Computer Science, but I also took many classes of Graphic Design and Fine Arts in the University, which gave me strong design foundations.

I started my professional career as Motion Designer followed by Frontend Developer and now I'm a happy Experience Designer, working with digital products.
